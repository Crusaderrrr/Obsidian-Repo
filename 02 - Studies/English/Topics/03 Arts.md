**Amateur** :: lacking skill or not done in a professional way 
**Authentic** :: real and not copy ("Authentic Mexican sombrero")
**Decorative** :: intended to look attractive rather than being of practical use 
**Symbolic** :: having an occulted meaning 
**Auction house** :: a company in the business of selling items at auction
**Bronze** :: a material (can also be a statue)
**Ceramic** :: an object such as a pot, bowl, etc. that is made by heating clay
**Canvas** :: an oil painting done on a canvas
**Mural** :: a large painting done on a wall
**Oil painting** :: the art or technique of painting with oil paint\
**Silhouette** ::  drawing that depicts the outline of someone or something that is in a single black color and against a light background, often from the side
**Still life** :: painting that represents stationary objects 
**Print** :: a picture or design created by pressing an engraved surface onto a paper
**Depth** :: a three dimensional aspect 
**Finish** :: the last layer that protects the painting 
**Harmony** :: a pleasing combination of things in a way that forms a coherent whole 
**Patron** :: an individual who financially supports an artist, charity, etc.
**Sculptor**::  someone who makes works of art by carving or shaping wood, clay, metal, etc.
**Palette** :: a thin oval where color are being mixed 
**Viewpoint** :: a certain way thinking 
**Impressionism** :: movement in painting 
**Modernism** :: a style or movement in art
**Realism** :: another style
**Surrealism** :: style in art
**To carve** :: to shape or create by cutting or sculpting 
**To mold** :: to give a substance a particular shape 
**To pose** :: to maintain a specific posture in order to be photographed or painted 
**To shade** :: to darken part of a picture
