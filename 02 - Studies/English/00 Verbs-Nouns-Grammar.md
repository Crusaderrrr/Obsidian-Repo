# Words

**Unwind**
?
To relax after work or tension
**Synonyms**: relax, rest, de-stess
"It is important to unwind after a long day at work"
<!--SR:!2025-04-26,4,274-->

**Thrive**
?
To grow or develop well
**Synonyms**: flourish, prosper, succeed  
"Children thrive in a loving and supportive environment"

**Workforce**
?
All the people who work in a company or country
**Synonyms**: staff, employees, labor force
"A skilled workforce is a key to a company's success"
<!--SR:!2025-04-23,1,234-->

**Sector**
?
Parts or divisions of a larger group or area
**Synonyms**: divisions, segments, areas 
"The public sector employs many people in healthcare and education"

**Resent**
?
To feel angry or bitter about something 
**Synonyms**: begrudge, dislike, be annoyed by
"He began to resent the implication that he was not working hard enough"

**Revenue**
?
Income from business or government activities
**Synonyms**: income, earnings, profits
"The company's revenue has grown steadily"
<!--SR:!2025-04-23,1,234-->

**Proportion**

"A large proportion of the population supports the new law"
