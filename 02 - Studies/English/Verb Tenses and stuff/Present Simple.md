The **Present Simple** is used for habits, routines, facts, and things that are generally or always true. It’s also common for scheduled events and to express feelings or opinions. (often, usually, rarely)

- **Structure**: Subject + base verb (add *-s* or *-es* for third-person singular subjects).
- **Examples**:
    - "I study English every day." (habit)
