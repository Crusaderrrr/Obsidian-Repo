**Reported Speech** changes direct statements into indirect forms, adjusting tense, pronouns, and time expressions.
**When to Use**: To report what someone said or thought, common in Writing/Speaking tasks.
**Examples**:
Direct: “I will go,” she said. → Reported: She said that she would go.
Direct: “I’m studying,” he said. → Reported: He said that he was studying.
**Tip**: Backshift tenses (Present → Past, Will → Would) and change “now” to “then”—key for C1 accuracy!
