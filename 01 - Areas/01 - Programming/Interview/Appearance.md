- Look good or at least suitable for the office, appearance matters too 
- Use antiperspirant 
- Use unusual clothes, but not anything extreme 
- **Bring your own pen**
- Be prepared and don't stress 
- **If asked**: tell about your hobby, don't overreact or spend to much time. The hobby needs to be interesting. Good to something related to the job: programming contests, etc.
- Try to know as much as possible about the company, **know your opponent**.
- Don't lie.
- Find any possible link to the company and talk about this link during the interview.
- **Show positivity**
