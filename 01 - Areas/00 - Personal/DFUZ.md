This technique consists of: 
1. **Distinguish**
- You need to understand is that a feeling or a thought. Since we can’t control our feelings, we can, however, manage our thoughts. 
- You need to convert your “anxious” feelings into positive thoughts, like “yeah, I’m gonna do my best”
2.  **Fake** 
- “Fake it until you make it”
- I feel lazy or tired 
- I will pretend (act) like if I were full of energy 
- It leads to feel more motivated 
3. **Uptime** 
- We want to gradually increase the uptime of our thought-action defusion while counting to take action. 
- day 1 - 10 minutes of work 
- Day 2 - 12 minutes of work 
- And so forth 
4. **Zone** 
- create a zone free of distractions and procrastination triggers 
- Take a piece of paper and write down the cause of distraction
