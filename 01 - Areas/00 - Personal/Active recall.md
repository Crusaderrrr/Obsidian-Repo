#self_improvement 

*How It Works:*
1. Retrieve Information: Instead of re-reading notes or textbooks, you try to recall the key concepts or answers from memory.
2. Correct Errors: Compare your recall with the correct information to identify gaps in knowledge.
3. Repeat: Revisit challenging material and test yourself again, often using spaced repetition.

*Examples of Active Recall:*
• Using flashcards with questions on one side and answers on the other.
• Writing down everything you remember about a topic before checking your notes.
• Practicing with quizzes, questions, or past exams.
• Teaching a concept to someone else or explaining it aloud.
